Impressionism

Impressionism emerged in France during the late nineteenth century as artists began focusing on light, movement, and everyday life.

Claude Monet

Monet became one of the founders of the Impressionist movement through his focus on atmosphere and changing light.

Edgar Degas

Degas focused on movement, dancers, and scenes of modern Parisian life.
